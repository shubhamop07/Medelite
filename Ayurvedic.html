Ayurvedic Remedies

Ayurvedic Remedies for Fever

Tulsi: Tulsi (Holy Basil) has powerful anti-inflammatory properties. Boil tulsi leaves in water, add ginger and honey, and drink the mixture 2-3 times a day.

Giloy: Boil giloy stem in water, strain it, and drink the decoction. It helps boost immunity.

Honey and Ginger: A mixture of honey and ginger can reduce body temperature and soothe the throat.

Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of warm water and herbal teas to flush out toxins and stay hydrated.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Headache

Ginger Paste: Apply a paste of ginger and water on your forehead for 10-15 minutes to relieve pain.

Peppermint Oil: Massage your temples with peppermint oil to reduce tension headaches and improve blood circulation.

Clove: Crush a few cloves, place them in a cloth, and inhale the aroma for headache relief.

Hydration: Drink warm water with lemon and honey to prevent dehydration-induced headaches.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Cold and Cough

Turmeric Milk: Drink warm milk with turmeric (haldi) to alleviate cold and cough symptoms.

Tulsi and Ginger Tea: A tea made with tulsi, ginger, and honey is an excellent remedy for a sore throat and cough.

Steam Inhalation: Inhale steam from water boiled with eucalyptus oil or turmeric to clear nasal congestion.

Licorice Root: Chew licorice root or drink licorice tea to soothe the throat and relieve coughing.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Stomach Pain

Fennel Seeds (Saunf): Chew fennel seeds or drink fennel tea to relieve gas and bloating.

Ajwain: Mix ajwain seeds with black salt to treat stomach cramps and indigestion.

Ginger Tea: Drink ginger tea with honey to reduce inflammation and soothe stomach pain.

Buttermilk: Drink buttermilk with a pinch of asafoetida (hing) to aid digestion and relieve pain.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Body Pain

Turmeric Paste: Apply a paste of turmeric and coconut oil to the affected area for relief.

Mustard Oil Massage: Warm mustard oil and massage it onto the painful areas to improve circulation.

Ginger Tea: Drink ginger tea to help reduce inflammation and pain.

Eucalyptus Oil: Inhale eucalyptus oil to ease muscle and joint pain.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Eye Irritation

Cold Compress: Apply a cold compress to the eyes to reduce swelling and irritation.

Rose Water: Use rose water drops to soothe irritated eyes.

Triphala: Use Triphala water as an eye wash for relief from irritation.

Castor Oil: A drop of castor oil in each eye can help moisturize and reduce irritation.

Ayurvedic Remedies for BP & Sugar Control

Fenugreek Seeds: Soak fenugreek seeds overnight and consume them in the morning to help control blood sugar levels.

Curry Leaves: Chew a few curry leaves daily to help manage blood pressure and sugar.

Amla: Amla juice is beneficial for both blood pressure and blood sugar control.

Ginger Tea: Drinking ginger tea can also aid in regulating blood pressure.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Dog Bite

Cleansing: Clean the wound thoroughly with clean water.

Aloe Vera Gel: Apply fresh aloe vera gel to soothe the skin and prevent infection.

Turmeric Paste: Apply turmeric paste on the bite to promote healing and prevent infection.

Consult a Doctor: Always consult a healthcare provider for any bite to assess the need for a rabies vaccine.

Ayurvedic Remedies for Snake Bite

Immediate Medical Attention: Seek emergency medical help immediately.

Pulsatilla: This homeopathic remedy can be taken until medical help is available.

Application of Ice: Apply ice packs to the bitten area to reduce swelling.

Herbal Decoctions: Herbal decoctions like those made with neem leaves may help, but do not delay medical treatment.
